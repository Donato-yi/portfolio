About me

I am a passionate web developer with over 5 years of experience building websites and web applications. I specialize in front-end development and enjoy bringing designs to life in the browser using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I stay up-to-date on the latest web technologies and trends so I can create modern, responsive sites that provide a great user experience. I'm detail-oriented, driven to deliver high-quality work and committed to writing clean, maintainable code.

My ideal role allows me to exercise both my technical skills and creative problem-solving abilities. I thrive in an agile environment where collaboration and continuous learning are encouraged. In my previous roles, I've contributed to all phases of the development cycle, from discussing requirements with stakeholders to launching sites and fixing issues. Outside of work, I like to tinker with new JavaScript libraries and frameworks, read up on web design trends and contribute to open source projects. I'm excited to join a team where I can share my passion for web development and help build products that positively impact people's lives.
